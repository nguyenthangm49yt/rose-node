Dear my beloved,

On this special day of women,
I want to express my love and admiration,
For the incredible person that you are,

You are more than just a partner,
You are my best friend and confidant,
Your support and encouragement,
Have lifted me up and made me strong.

Your beauty radiates from within,
And your kind heart touches everyone,
You make me a better man,
And for that, I am forever grateful.

On this International Women's Day,

To me and to the world around us,
And I promise to always cherish you.
